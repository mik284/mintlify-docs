Lango is a web-based payment management portal that gives banks and financial institutions complete visibility and control over their POS terminal estate. From onboarding merchants and assigning terminals to monitoring live transactions and generating compliance reports, Lango puts every tool you need in a single, role-controlled interface.

## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the portal">
    Navigate to your Lango portal URL, enter your username and password, and complete the OTP verification step. See [Login & Authentication](/login-and-authentication) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    After login you land on the Dashboards section. Review the terminal telemetry map and transaction KPIs to get an immediate picture of your estate's health. See [Dashboard Overview](/dashboard/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Onboard your first customer">
    Head to **Customer Management**, create a new customer record, add their outlets, and assign POS terminals. Follow the guide at [Managing Customers](/customers/managing-customers).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review transactions and run reports">
    Use the **Transactions** section to search and filter payment records, then head to **Reports** to export compliance-ready data. Start with [Viewing Transactions](/transactions/viewing-transactions).
  </Step>
</Steps>
