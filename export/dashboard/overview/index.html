    Lango Dashboard: Real-time Terminal & Transaction Monitoring

    The Lango Dashboard gives you a live view of your terminal fleet and payment volumes. Access terminal telemetry and transaction KPIs from one screen.

    The Lango Dashboard is the command centre for your payment operations. After you log in, the platform lands you here automatically — no extra navigation required. From a single screen you can monitor the health of every POS terminal in your estate and review payment performance across your entire network. The Dashboard is split into two focused views: Terminals Telemetry and Transactions Dashboard. Each view is purpose-built so your operations team can immediately act on what they see.

    ​
    What the Dashboard Shows

    The Dashboard presents two complementary views of your payment estate:
    • Terminals Telemetry — live connectivity status, temperature readings, PIN pad health, and a GPS cluster map of every terminal in your fleet.
    • Transactions Dashboard — aggregate payment volumes, success and decline rates, monthly trends, and a currency-level breakdown of total transaction amounts.
    Both views refresh automatically in the background, so the figures you see always reflect the current state of your network.

    ​
    Navigating the Dashboard

    The Dashboard is the default landing page after login. You can also return to it at any time by selecting Dashboards from the left-hand sidebar. The platform automatically lands you on the Terminals Telemetry view first. To switch to the Transactions Dashboard, select Transactions Dashboard from the sidebar under Dashboards.

    ​
    Dashboard Views

    Terminals Telemetry

    Monitor live connectivity, temperature, PIN pad status, and GPS locations for every terminal in your fleet.

    Transactions Dashboard

    Track real-time payment volumes, success and decline rates, and monthly transaction trends across your network.

    ​
    Access Controls

    Your administrator controls which views are available to your account through role-based permissions. The two dashboard views are gated independently:
    PermissionWhat it unlocks
    canViewTerminalDashboardAccess to the Terminals Telemetry view and all terminal detail drill-downs
    canViewSystemDashboardAccess to the Transactions Dashboard view
    If a tab is greyed out or not visible in your sidebar, your account has not been granted the corresponding permission. Contact your system administrator to request access.
